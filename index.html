The Briefing

Every heist needs a plan.

PRARAMBH — meaning "the beginning" — is the flagship symposium of the Christ University Commerce Association (CUCA), bringing together the sharpest minds of the Department of Commerce for a single afternoon of strategy, capital, and nerve.

This year's operation is styled after the world's most famous heist: red jumpsuits, a professor's plan, and a vault worth cracking. Seven rooms. Seven skills. One shot at the gold.

Participants register individually and are assigned to one of seven domains — each a test designed by a different "cell" of the crew. Come prepared. The Bank of Spain didn't fall to luck.
