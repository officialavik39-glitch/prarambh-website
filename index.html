The Briefing

Every heist needs a plan.

PRARAMBH —"The Beginning"—is the flagship symposium of the Christ University Commerce Association (CUCA), bringing together the sharpest minds of the Department of Commerce for an afternoon where strategy, skill, and ambition are put to the ultimate test.

This year's operation draws inspiration from the world's most iconic heist. Behind the masks, the red jumpsuits, and the Professor's blueprint lies a vault unlike any other one built on commerce. Seven rooms. Seven domains. Seven champions waiting to emerge.

Each room is dedicated to a different discipline of commerce, testing the skills, decisions, and teamwork needed to conquer it. Choose your domain, enlist for the operation, and prove you have what it takes to outthink the competition.
